# Market Research

## Summary

There are existing products that prove the core interaction is valid: tap-to-translate reading, vocabulary capture, and contextual explanation. None of the reviewed products fully matches this project because this project is a private technical manual with fixed Chinese-English page alignment and domain-specific Six Sigma terminology.

## Reviewed Products

### Readlang

Source: https://readlang.com/

Relevant pattern:

- click a word or phrase to translate
- upload user-owned texts
- review translated words with flashcards

Gap for this project:

- not designed around fixed-page bilingual technical manuals
- no custom Six Sigma term layer by default
- not optimized for preserving manual figures and page mapping

### LingQ

Source: https://www.lingq.com/en/

Relevant pattern:

- import content
- tap words while reading
- save vocabulary for review
- local progress and vocabulary growth are central product concepts

Gap for this project:

- general language-learning flow, not a private professional-certification manual
- domain explanations would need custom terms anyway

### Language Reactor

Source: https://www.languagereactor.com/

Relevant pattern:

- instant word translation
- example sentences
- contextual explanations
- language learning while consuming native content

Gap for this project:

- strongest for video/subtitle workflows
- not a document/manual reader

### Kindle / E-reader Dictionary + Vocabulary Builder

Reference: Kindle vocabulary builder behavior is widely used in e-reader workflows; lookup creates a saved vocabulary list and keeps usage context.

Relevant pattern:

- tap word to see dictionary
- save looked-up words
- show usage context from book

Gap for this project:

- weak phrase handling
- weak bilingual page alignment
- no custom Six Sigma term database

## Product Opportunity

Build a private, manual-specific study reader:

- one-tap Chinese/English switch on the same page and paragraph
- English reading mode with word-level and phrase-level lookup
- Six Sigma terminology layer ahead of general dictionary lookup
- vocabulary book with source page, chapter, sentence, and review status
- preserved technical figures and tables

## Differentiation

The product is not competing with general language-learning platforms. It is a personal technical-study tool: the content, terminology, page numbers, and examples come from one manual and are optimized for exam preparation and practical review.
