# Product Requirements

## Product

Six Sigma Study App is a private mobile-first bilingual reader for the Six Sigma Black Belt manual.

## Target User

A Chinese-speaking learner preparing for Six Sigma Black Belt study who wants to read the English original while quickly checking Chinese meaning, technical terms, and saved vocabulary.

## Core Jobs

- Read the English manual without breaking concentration.
- Switch to Chinese instantly when a paragraph is hard.
- Understand Six Sigma terms in context, not only dictionary meanings.
- Save important words and phrases into a vocabulary book.
- Return to a word's original page and sentence later.

## MVP Scope

### Reader

- chapter list
- current chapter reader
- sticky or floating Chinese/English switch
- preserve current paragraph and scroll position when switching
- page number display

### Lookup

- tap any English word
- bottom half-screen explanation sheet
- word, translation, part of speech, explanation, source sentence
- Six Sigma term badge where applicable
- fallback state for words not yet in the dictionary

### Phrase Support

- long-press or drag-select phrase lookup
- phrase dictionary has priority over single-word lookup
- examples: process improvement, control chart, voice of the customer

### Vocabulary Book

- save word or phrase
- source chapter/page/paragraph
- source sentence
- review status: new, learning, known
- basic filtering by term type

### Content

- Chinese and English paragraph pairs
- preserved figures and table images
- stable page mapping from the 449-page print edition

## Non-goals For MVP

- public app-store release
- user account and cloud sync
- full AI tutor
- payment/subscription
- OCR inside the app

## Success Criteria

- First chapter can be studied end-to-end on a phone.
- English and Chinese modes keep the same reading position.
- Every visible English word can be tapped.
- Known Six Sigma terms show curated technical explanations.
- Saved vocabulary persists locally after refresh.
- Chapter/page mapping remains aligned with the print edition.
